Smoochum is a Baby Pokémon of the Ice and Psychic types, introduced during the second generation of Pokémon games. It is officially classified as the Kiss Pokémon, a title that speaks directly to its most prominent physical feature. Smoochum is a small, bipedal creature with a distinctly humanoid silhouette, standing well below knee height on an average adult trainer. Its body is soft and rounded, built around a compact torso and a notably oversized head. The skin across its body is a soft, uniform pink, and its most immediately recognizable feature is a pair of large, prominently puckered lips set at the center of its face, rendered in a deeper rose shade that immediately draws the eye. Framing that face is a cascade of long, flowing blonde hair that fans outward from the crown of its head, and its eyes are wide, round, and a vivid purple. The overall impression is that of a small, expressive figure whose face communicates both curiosity and gentleness.

Smoochum tends to appear in urban and suburban environments, gravitating toward areas where humans live and congregate rather than seeking out remote wilderness. Despite its Ice typing, it does not naturally migrate toward frigid climates; it is more at home in temperate, populated zones where its highly sensitive lips can engage with a rich variety of textures and surfaces. In the wild, it is an uncommon find even within its preferred habitats, and population density tends to stay low wherever it is encountered. The species is primarily associated with the Johto region, though urban sightings have been reported more broadly. Smoochum does not display strong migratory patterns, remaining largely settled in a given territory throughout the year. It is active during daylight hours and does not follow a strictly nocturnal routine, making it more accessible to daytime trainers on the lookout for rare encounters.

Smoochum is a profoundly sensory-driven Pokémon, and its behavior is organized almost entirely around the remarkable sensitivity of its lips. When it encounters any unfamiliar object, surface, or creature, its first and most consistent instinct is to press its lips against it, gathering detailed tactile information that other Pokémon might acquire through sight, sound, or smell. This lip-first investigation method is so characteristic that field researchers treat it as a reliable behavioral signature of the species. Beyond its exploratory habits, Smoochum is affectionate and attentive toward those it trusts, and it has been documented frequently checking its own reflection in still water or any nearby shiny surface, a behavior that many trainers find both charming and telling. Its diet consists of sweet, soft foods that it can experience fully through its sensitive mouth. It generally avoids conflict when possible, preferring engagement over confrontation, though it carries a quiet self-assurance that grows more apparent as it matures.

In battle, Smoochum draws on two standard abilities and one hidden ability that together shape a surprisingly thoughtful combat profile. The first prevents it from falling under the effect of infatuation-based moves and shields it from attacks that exploit attraction, keeping its focus completely undivided during a fight. The second is perhaps its most tactically interesting capability: upon entering a battle, Smoochum instinctively reads its opponent and identifies the most powerful move available to that Pokémon, broadcasting that information to its trainer before a single exchange has taken place. This intelligence advantage can reshape an entire battle plan in an instant. Its hidden ability grants it the power to shake off any persistent status ailment at the end of each turn when the weather is rainy, adding a layer of resilience that is easy to underestimate. As a combined Ice and Psychic type, Smoochum has meaningful offensive reach but faces pressure from a wide range of attack types including Fighting, Rock, Steel, Fire, Bug, Ghost, and Dark. Its strength lies in special attacks, and it performs best in situations where its information-gathering instincts can be put to immediate strategic use.

Smoochum is the pre-evolved form of Jynx, the Humanshape Pokémon of the first generation. It evolves into Jynx upon reaching a sufficient level of experience. Because Smoochum is classified as a Baby Pokémon, it cannot be produced through ordinary breeding; Jynx must hold a specific item in the Pokémon Nursery in order to produce a Smoochum egg rather than another Jynx. This requirement makes Smoochum a meaningful objective for breeders and collectors who wish to trace Jynx back to its earliest form. Within the broader Pokédex, Smoochum holds a place among the Baby Pokémon introduced in the second generation specifically to add developmental history to first-generation species. Researchers studying sensory development in Pokémon are drawn to Smoochum in particular because its lip-based cognition offers a rare window into how tactile intelligence precedes and complements the emergence of full psychic ability. For trainers willing to invest time before evolving it, Smoochum provides one of the more uniquely tactile bonding experiences the Pokédex has to offer.
